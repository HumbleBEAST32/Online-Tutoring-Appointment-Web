JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL

Key Stage Standards:

At the end of grade 10, the student demonstrates understanding and appreciation of key concepts and skills involving numbers and number sense (sets and real numbers); measurement (conversion of units); patterns and algebra (linear equations and inequalities in one and two variables, linear functions, systems of linear equations, and inequalities in two variables, exponents and radicals, quadratic equations, inequalities, functions, polynomials, and polynomial equations and functions); geometry (polygons, axiomatic structure of geometry, triangle congruence, inequality and similarity, and basic trigonometry); statistics and probability (measures of central tendency, variability, and position; combinatorics and probability) as applied, using appropriate technology, in critical thinking, problem solving, communicating, reasoning, making connections, representations, and decisions in real life.

Grade 7

    Number & Algebra:
  • Rational numbers and exponents
  • Algebraic expressions and linear equations
    Geometry:
  • Congruence and similarity
  • Basic constructions using compasses and rulers
    Statistics:
  • Stem-and-leaf plots, histograms, and mean/median/mode review
    Measurement:
  • Area and circumference of circles, volume of solids

Grade 8

    Algebra:
  • Linear functions and graphing on coordinate plane
  • Systems of equations (intro)
    Geometry:
  • Pythagorean theorem
  • Transformations (translations, reflections, rotations)
    Data Analysis:
  • Scatter plots and basic correlation
    Number Theory:
  • Irrational numbers and square roots

Grade 9

    Algebra:
  • Quadratic equations and functions
  • Polynomials and factoring
    Geometry:
  • Proofs involving congruence and similarity
  • Trigonometry (sine, cosine, tangent basics)
    Statistics & Probability:
  • Probability rules and simple events
  • Interpreting data distributions

Grade 10

    Advanced Algebra:
  • Rational expressions and equations
  • Radical expressions and complex numbers (intro)
    Trigonometry:
  • Solving right triangles, unit circle basics
    Geometry:
  • Circles, tangents, and secants
    Statistics & Probability:
  • Standard deviation, normal distribution (intro)
  • Compound probability
