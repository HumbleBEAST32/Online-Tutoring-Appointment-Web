Procedure:

1. Click/tap Registration.
2. Choose your current Grade Level.
3. Once you get to the specified registration tab of your choice, fill up the form and click/tap Register.
4. Verify your email address.
5. Once you are done verifying your email, click/tap the Login at the upper right corner.
6. Proceed by logging in your account by clicking/tapping Login.
7. Once done logging in, your dashboard will appear.


Requirements:

  • Must be on the specified grade levels
  • The worktext/workbook that you are using must have a copy that the tutor can purchase online/in physical stores for smoother guidance while in the tutorial session.
  • A device (e.g., PC, Smartphone, Tablet) that can run Google Meet.
  • A webcam/front camera for the tutor to see if you are paying attention.
  • A software/application (e.g., Paint, Canva Tools) that will let you write and show your answers to your tutor while in the tutorial session.
